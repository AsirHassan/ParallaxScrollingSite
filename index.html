Welcome to Car Assembly

Experience the journey from metal to machine.

The Foundation of Strength

Every great vehicle begins with a strong and precise chassis. This is the skeleton of the car, the structural framework that supports every other component. In this stage, metal frames are cut, shaped, and welded to create the backbone that ensures durability, safety, and performance. It’s where form meets function.

Fun Fact: The chassis must pass strict stress and crash tests before moving to the next stage!

Powering the Machine

Now it’s time to give the car its heart, the engine. Engine installation is a highly technical phase where precision is key. The powertrain, gearbox, and essential electronics are fitted with care and accuracy. This stage transforms the car from a hollow shell into a roaring machine, capable of delivering speed, efficiency, and control.

Quote: "This is where the car gets its soul."

Bringing Beauty to Life

It’s more than just color, it’s personality. Each vehicle enters a multi-step paint booth process: primer, basecoat, and clear coat, all applied in a dust-free, temperature-controlled environment. This stage isn’t just about aesthetics. It protects the car from rust, wear, and weather and gives it that iconic showroom shine.

Highlight: Paint robots apply every layer with flawless accuracy. Human hands add custom detailing.

Precision in the Details

This is where the magic happens. The interiors, dashboards, lighting systems, infotainment units, mirrors, tires, and more come together. Every bolt, every stitch, every switch is carefully installed and tested. The car undergoes rigorous quality checks, from brake systems to infotainment displays. It’s no longer a project. It’s a finished product, ready for the road.

Did You Know? Each vehicle goes through over 1,000 checks before it rolls out.
